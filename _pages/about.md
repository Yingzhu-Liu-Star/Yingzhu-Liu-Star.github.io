I'm a second-year Ph.D. student in Dynamical Systems and Control with the Department of Mechanics and Engineering Science at the [College of Engineering](https://www.coe.pku.edu.cn) at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn), under the supervision of [Prof. Zhongkui Li](https://www.zhongkuili-pku.com) and [Prof. Pengcheng You](https://pengcheng-you.github.io/desires-lab/people.html). Prior to that, I acquired my Bachelor's degree majoring in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics, at the [College of Engineering](https://www.coe.pku.edu.cn), [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn).

Research Interests
======
My current research interests include distributed robust and optimal control, saddle flow dynamics and reinforcement learning.
